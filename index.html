I am currently a final-year Ph.D student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at HKUST. My supervisors are Dr. Sunghun KIM and Dr. Qifeng Chen. I graduated from Peking University in 2020 with a bachelor's degree.

Email  /  Google Scholar

Research

My research focuses on the intersection of AI and Society. On one hand, I work on socially responsible AI , prioritizing aspects such as safety, reliability, and privacy. On the other hand, I investigate the societal implication of AI, including education, healthcare, equality, and more.

Topics I currently focus on:

  • Socially Responsible LLMs: Jailbreak-Defense, GradSafe, BIPIA , MLLM-Protector
  • Secure and Robust Federated Learning: SmartFL, DynaFed
  • Societal implication of AI.

Others:

  • Recommender Systems: REMI, DIF-SR, Atten-Mixer, ECL-SR
  • Image Compression: InvCompress, IICNet, DenoiseCompression
