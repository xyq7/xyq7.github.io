Yueqi Xie 谢悦琪

I am currently a final-year Ph.D student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at HKUST. My supervisors are Dr. Sunghun KIM and Dr. Qifeng Chen. I graduated from Peking University in 2020 with a bachelor's degree.

My research focuses on building trustworthy AI systems. I'm broadly interested in security, robustness, and effectiveness of Federated Learning, Recommender Systems, Computer Vision, and Large Language Models.

I was an intern in MSRA, working with Dr. Fangzhao Wu and Dr. Xing Xie. I'm fortunate to work with Dr. Minghong Fang and Dr. Neil Gong.

I'm looking for postdoc position starting from 2024 Spring/Summer with a research focus on trustworthy AI. Please drop me an email if you think I would be a good fit for your team. Thanks!
